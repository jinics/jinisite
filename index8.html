Introduction

This project was Exhibition Branding for IY1 Graphic design web exhibition. The Identity of our brand is to make a media to share experiences and to make connections between art works and the users. Also, to increase the engaging and inspiration, providing new knowledge and contents.

Logo

Phoenix": A simple logotype with custom letterforms gave way to developing a modern, minimal and sophisticated visual language that speaks to form, function and design. The visual language uses elements taken directly from the logotype. This language speaks to the three key areas being form, function and design.
