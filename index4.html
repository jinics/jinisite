Kenzo 2020ss Poster

Introduction

This project is to promote Kenzo 2020ss launch event. The theme of the 2020ss was "Japan’s ocean waters". Also, Groups of Japanese female freedivers known as the Ama – who have dived for over 2000 years to forage for seafood and pearls for their communities

Concept and Inspiration

The concept of this project was ‘Heliography’ and ‘Horizon’. From the showcase video, the visual that was used for deco of the show were ‘Heliography’ and ‘Horizon’. From that, this design was to show the vibe of water and horizon. Using warm colour to express the light and the image of horizon.
