Kala Music Festival Promote

Introduction

This project is making a promotion poster, flyer, online banner and badge for the festival. The concept of this design is 'freedome', 'Summer' and 'cool'. Using bright and cool colour to make the audience feel coolest by just looking at the design.

Concept and target audience

As the target audience ios age around 20-30 and People who want to experience new thing is music festival. The concept is “summer coolness” .The concept of the festival is holiday and chill

Design elements

Using a combination of colorful colors and lowers the saturation of the warm colors, making the cool and warm feelings coexist. Shape of flow and natural images. A selection in the circle are different parts of place of Albanian.
